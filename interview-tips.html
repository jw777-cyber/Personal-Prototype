Interview Checklist

✅ Before
Research company, practice questions, plan outfit, arrive early.
🎯 During
Listen actively, STAR method, ask thoughtful questions.
📩 After
Thank-you email, follow-up within 24h.
⚠️ Avoid
Negative talk, interrupting, unprepared answers.
